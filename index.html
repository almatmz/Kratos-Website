Young Kratos - The Ghost of Sparta

Driven by Vengeance

The younger Kratos is consumed by rage and the desire for revenge against the gods who betrayed him. After being tricked into killing his own family by Ares, he becomes the Ghost of Sparta, marked by their ashes.

Weapons & Powers

  • Blades of Chaos - Chained weapons forged in the depths of Hades
  • Spartan Rage - Uncontrollable fury that grants immense power
  • God-slaying abilities gained through his journey
  • Incredible strength and brutal combat skills

Character Traits

  • Ruthless and brutal in combat
  • Single-minded focus on revenge
  • Willing to sacrifice anything for power
  • Haunted by his past sins
  • Unforgiving towards enemies and gods alike

Old Kratos - The Father

Seeking Redemption

The older Kratos has learned from his past mistakes. Now a father to Atreus, he struggles to control his rage and be a better man. He carries the weight of his actions and seeks to break the cycle of violence.

Weapons & Powers

  • Leviathan Axe - A powerful weapon imbued with frost
  • Controlled Spartan Rage - Channeled fury with purpose
  • Guardian Shield - Defensive capabilities and strategic combat
  • Wisdom gained from experience and loss

Character Growth

  • Protective and caring father figure
  • Strives to control his violent nature
  • Teaches wisdom and restraint to his son
  • Haunted but learning to forgive himself
  • Seeks to be a better role model
